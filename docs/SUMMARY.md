# Summary

A cheat sheet for those familiar with TTRPGs or who need a reminder of TND's system.


## Stats

Stat    | Meaning
------- | ------------
Timbre  | Intelligence
Tune    | Emotion
Tempo   | Quickness
Timpani | Power

Bonus to checks from 1-8.


## Motifs

Several specific skills that your character is proficient in.
Chosen at character creation, more gained with time.
Bonus to checks from 1-5.


## Checks

2d6 rolls made against a check value. Get that value or
higher to pass.

Add the relevant stat, motif (if any) and any modifiers your DM
tells you to add.


## Combat

Action   | Description
-------- | -----------
Attack   | Target a person.
Defend   | Prepare for another's action.
Interact | Target the environment.
