# Characters

## Improvement

Improvement does not happen all at once; Kai Zen. As you interact with the
world, if the DM notices that you are exercising a particular aspect
frequently and successfully, they might decide to improve your character's
ability. This can happen in two ways:

- **Motif improvements.** If a character improves at a skill or enhances
their understanding of a concept, this is likely to be reflected in one of their
motifs. This can either result in an increase in the bonus of a motif (by 1)
or in the gaining of a new motif to be discussed with the DM.
- **Stat improvements.** These happen much more rarely than motif improvements,
and tend to signify a substantial shift in a character's ability or personality.
Hand out with care, as this has a significant impact on what the character is able 
to do.
