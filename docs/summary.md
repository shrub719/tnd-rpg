# Summary


## [Stats](./stats.md)

**PIQuE:**

- **Power**
- **Intellect**
- **Quickness**
- **Emotion**

Bonus to checks from 1-8.

[Items](./items.md) add to your stats or motifs while wearing them.


## [Checks](./checks.md)

2d6 rolls made against a check threshold. Get that value or
higher to pass. Passive checks are calculated as if you
rolled a 6.

Add the relevant stat, motif (if any) and any modifiers your DM
tells you to add.


## [Combat](./combat.md)

When entering combat, you choose your key (stat used for
checks, defense and leitmotifs).

Each turn consists of a single action.

Action    | Description
--------- | -----------
Attack    | Directly target an enemy with a check.
Interact  | Manipulate the environment or use an item.
Leitmotif | Use a leitmotif under the right conditions.

Turns are popcorned between players, starting with the initiator.
Each character goes once per round, and then the opposing side acts.


## [Characters](./characters.md)

Your character sheet should have:

- Stats
- Motifs
- Leitmotifs
- Items
- Character details (appearance, backstory)
